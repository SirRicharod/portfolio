I am a software developer based in Belgium with a background in game design and a focus on full-stack web applications.

My experience in game design shaped how I approach development. I view applications through a player-first lens: every interaction should be intuitive, rewarding, and purposeful. In game development, you learn quickly that if a mechanic is frustrating, the player leaves. I carry those same standards over into every web application I develop.

I focus on turning complex technical requirements into intuitive experiences. My goal is to bridge the gap between back-end logic and front-end usability, ensuring that every part of an application works together to feel helpful and efficient rather than a hurdle for the user to overcome.
